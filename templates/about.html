Our Story

Built for Empathy.
Powered by Intelligence.

EmpathyFirst was founded with a singular mission: to make mental health triage faster, more accurate, and deeply human — by giving clinicians AI they can trust.

Our Mission

Closing the Gap in Mental Health Care

Over 970 million people worldwide live with a mental health condition. Yet, early identification remains the single biggest obstacle to effective treatment. Clinicians spend hours reviewing notes, listening to recordings, and reading between the lines.

EmpathyFirst changes that. Our multi-modal AI analyzes voice stress patterns, facial micro-expressions, and clinical narratives simultaneously — giving doctors a real-time picture of a patient's emotional state in seconds, not hours.

We are not replacing the doctor. We are giving them a superpower.

What We Stand For

Core Values

Everything we build is guided by a set of principles that put humans first and technology second.

❤️

Human-Centered Design

Every feature is designed around the clinical workflow and the emotional needs of vulnerable patients — not the other way around.

🔍

Radical Transparency

Our AI explains its reasoning at every step. No black boxes — only explainable, auditable decisions that clinicians can trust and challenge.

🛡️

Privacy by Default

We process data at the edge, never store videos or audio, and comply fully with HIPAA and GDPR from day one. Trust is not a feature, it's infrastructure.

⚖️

Equitable Access

Mental health care is a right, not a privilege. We build multilingual, multi-cultural tools to serve every patient — regardless of language or location.

🔬

Evidence-Based AI

Every model we ship is validated against peer-reviewed clinical research. We move fast, but never at the expense of clinical precision.

🤝

Collaborative Innovation

We partner with hospitals, researchers, and patient advocacy groups to co-build tools that solve real problems — from the inside out.
